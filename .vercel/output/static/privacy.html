Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026  ·  ShopBG Remover (operated by shopbgremover)

We keep this simple: we collect only what's necessary to run the service, we don't sell your data, and your product images are never stored after processing is complete.

1. Who We Are

ShopBG Remover is an AI-powered background removal tool for e-commerce sellers, operated under the brand shopbgremover. You can reach us at yubudong2023@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information needed to provide the service:

  • Account information: Your email address and display name when you sign in via Google OAuth or email OTP.
  • Usage data: Number of images processed, timestamps, and credit balance — to manage your account and enforce plan limits.
  • Payment information: Order ID, plan purchased, and payment amount. We do not store your card number or full PayPal credentials — payments are handled entirely by PayPal.
  • Technical data: Browser type, IP address, and access logs collected automatically by Cloudflare for security and performance purposes.

3. Your Images

Your product images are sent to our AI processing API solely for background removal. Images are not stored on our servers after processing is complete. Processed results are returned directly to your browser and are never retained or used for any other purpose.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and manage your account
  • To track credit usage and process payments
  • To send OTP verification codes via email (using Resend)
  • To respond to support inquiries
  • To detect and prevent abuse

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate ShopBG Remover:

  • Cloudflare — hosting, CDN, DNS, and DDoS protection
  • Google OAuth — optional sign-in method; governed by Google's Privacy Policy
  • PayPal — payment processing; governed by PayPal's Privacy Policy
  • Resend — transactional email delivery (OTP codes)

6. Cookies & Local Storage

We use a session cookie (session) to keep you logged in. It is set on .shopbgremover.com and expires after 30 days. We also use browser localStorage to track free usage limits for anonymous users. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account and usage records for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing yubudong2023@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other applicable laws, including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at yubudong2023@gmail.com.

9. Children's Privacy

ShopBG Remover is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, email us at yubudong2023@gmail.com.
