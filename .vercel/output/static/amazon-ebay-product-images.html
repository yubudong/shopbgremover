Marketplace image policies

Exactly what Amazon & eBay require

Non-compliant images get suppressed or removed from search. ShopBG Remover outputs images that meet both platforms' policies by default.

🟠
Amazon MAIN Image Policy
  • Pure white background — RGB (255, 255, 255)
  • Product occupies at least 85% of the image area
  • No text, logos, or watermarks
  • Minimum 1000×1000 px (for zoom feature)
  • Maximum 10,000 px on longest side
  • JPEG, PNG, GIF, or TIFF format
Listings with non-white backgrounds can be suppressed from search results.
🔴
eBay Listing Image Standards
  • Minimum 500×500 px recommended
  • White or neutral background preferred
  • No borders, watermarks, or overlaid text
  • Product clearly visible and well-lit
  • Up to 24 photos per listing
  • JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP, or TIFF
Clean white background images consistently achieve higher click-through rates on eBay.

ShopBG Remover outputs pure white (RGB 255,255,255) backgrounds which meet Amazon's MAIN image policy. This eliminates the most common reason for image suppression: non-white backgrounds. Always verify your listing complies with Amazon's full image requirements after uploading.
Can I process an entire Amazon catalog at once?
Yes — upload up to 50 images per batch. For large catalogs, you can run multiple batches. All output images are delivered as a single ZIP file named to match your originals.
Does it work for FBA product photos taken at home?
Yes. Many FBA sellers photograph products on a desk, floor, or with a colored backdrop. ShopBG Remover replaces any background with a compliant white background in seconds.
What about eBay's multiple image requirement?
eBay allows up to 24 images per listing. You can batch process all angle shots and variations at once, getting a full set of clean white background images for each listing in one go.
