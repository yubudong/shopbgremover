Shopify image guidelines

What Shopify requires for product images

Shopify recommends specific image standards to maximize conversions and ensure your store looks professional across all devices.

📐
Size & Resolution
  • 2048×2048 px recommended (square)
  • Minimum 800×800 px
  • Maximum 4472×4472 px
  • Under 20 MB per image
🎨
Background
  • White or light neutral background
  • Consistent across all products
  • No shadows or gradients on main image
  • Product fills 80–90% of frame
📁
File Format
  • JPG (best for white backgrounds)
  • PNG (if you need transparency)
  • WebP supported
  • GIF for animated product views
